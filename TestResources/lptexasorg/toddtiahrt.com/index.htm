Todd Tiahrt

"Doing the right thing"

Todd Tiahrt grew up on his family's farm where, at a young age, he learned the values of family and faith. Todd began serving the 4th District of Kansas in Congress in 1995. He was named to the powerful House Appropriations Committee in 1997. Todd has earned his reputation as a champion of taxpayers and those who believe in traditional American values.

As a member of Congress, Todd Tiahrt's voting record has received endorsement from respected organizations such as the U.S. Chamber of Commerce, the National Federation of Independent Business and Citizens Against Government Waste. Todd has also been named a Super Friend of Seniors, Champion of Private Property Rights and a Taxpayer's Hero.

Todd and his wife Vicki were blessed with three children.
