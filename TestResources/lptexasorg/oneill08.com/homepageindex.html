A Welcome Message from Bill

Now is the time.

Everywhere I go the past few months I’ve met folks who are tired of the way Washington does business. They’re tired of the leaders we send to Congress talking a good game, but leaving Northeast Ohio behind when push comes to shove. And most of all they’re tired of watching the nightly news and seeing their country continue to head in the wrong direction.

I’m running to change all that.

The people of Northeast Ohio deserve a congressman who will fight for them and will never forget that what’s on the minds of everyday people back at home is more important than the agenda of any political party or talking heads inside the Beltway.

I’m running because bringing change to Washington isn’t going to happen unless we change the people we send there.

And I need your help. Please sign up to join the campaign and find out how you can help make this change. Take a minute to read about me and where I stand, and – most importantly – let me know what’s on your mind.

My best,
