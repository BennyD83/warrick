Shrink Iowa's Government - Litten for Governor

We need to shrink Iowa's Government because,
each new government program:
  • is subject to politics
  • suffers from poor or indifferent administration
  • ends up costing more than originally planned
  • fails to work any better than the last one
  • has unintended negative consequences
  • will ultimately be misused

Other issues

  • Iowa citizens should be able to propose ballot initiatives. Our constitution gives us this right,why can't we do it?
  • I support an individuals right to protect themself, the Castle Doctrine, and "Shall Issue" policies such as those proposed by www.iowacarry.org
  • 12 States have no property tax, shouldn't Iowa be the 13th?
  • I support open debates between all candidates - not these phony forums sponsored by special interests and biased media. Having only two parties offers Iowa the same choice, which is no choice.
  • Iowa's charter school law essentially bans charter schools, it needs to be changed. Our students need choices, not restrictions.
  • There must be transparency in government. With the internet there is no excuse for not publishing where all our money goes.
  • There must be a hard copy for all votes cast. Touch screen voting can be hacked and will be faked. It is too easy to abuse.
