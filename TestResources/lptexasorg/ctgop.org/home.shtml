Welcome to the Connecticut Republican Party's website! Our purpose here at State Central is very simple. WE HELP ELECT REPUBLICANS! It is our hope that this website will assist Republicans and other like-minded individuals become more informed about our Party. We offer support to candidates, town committees and anyone interested in the Republican Party. We encourage you to participate in any way you can: sign up to Volunteer and become active in your local town committee, Donate to the party and help Republicans win seats in offices across the State of Connecticut from Mayors and First Selectmen to Governor of this great State.

Republicans are the Party of values, integrity and freedom. Help me inform the people of this State all that our Party has to offer…PLEASE GET INVOLVED!

If I can be of assistance to you, please contact me by E-mail at chairman@ctgop.org or by telephone at 860.547.0589.

Sincerely,

William A. Hamzy, Chairman
Connecticut Republican Party


Whether you are already involved or you would like to become active, please consider attending local Party events in your area and joining your local town committee.

You will find more information about these meetings in our calendar of events or by contacting your local chairman of the Party.

We hope you will return to this web site often as this site is updated frequently.
